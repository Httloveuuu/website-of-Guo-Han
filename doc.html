ETHICS SCENARIO

You work for a major information technology company. You have been authorized to hire a technical assistant and are about to prepare an advertisement. You are seeking this assistant during a time of threatened cutbacks for your company. People hired as “temporary,” however, have never seemed to work out well.

1. From a company standpoint, what are the pros and cons of advertising the position as temporary?

2. From a potential employee standpoint, what are the pros and cons of being informed that the job may be temporary? Would you apply for the job?

3. As an employee of the company, should you include a warning that this position could be only temporary? Why or why not? Consider your decision from the standpoint of the technical assistant whose job it is to write an advertisement that will draw applicants to the position.

4. How might you apply the strategies from the textbook to this case ? What are the ethical dilemmas you faced in this scenario?
